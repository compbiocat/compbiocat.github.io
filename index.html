CompBioCat @ IQCC

We are part of the Institute of Computational Chemistry and Catalysis (IQCC), combining quantum chemistry, molecular simulations and bioinformatics to engineer new enzymes and understand biochemical reactivity.

The IQCC is an international benchmark in computational chemistry and catalysis. Our group contributes to the institute's three lines of research, which emphasize computational chemistry tools, sustainable catalysis, and predictive chemistry in the confined space.
