Our Research

The CompBioCat group focuses on the development and application of computational methods — including quantum chemistry, multiscale modeling, and machine learning — to study and engineer enzymatic catalysis and biochemical reactivity. Our goal is to provide atomic-level understanding and rational design strategies for enzymes relevant in synthetic biology, biotechnology, and green chemistry.
